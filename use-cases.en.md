## ✅ Use case 1 — Finance platform modernization

**Problem**: critical legacy platform, tight coupling, slow deployments, recurring incidents.  
**Approach**: architecture blueprint, domain decoupling, phased migration to cloud‑ready services.  
**Outcome**: stabilized operations, faster releases, stronger DevSecOps governance.

---

## ✅ Use case 2 — Security & IAM for an insurance platform

**Problem**: sensitive B2B flows, compliance requirements, fragmented IAM.  
**Approach**: OWASP/IAM audit, SSO/OAuth2 rollout, access hardening and secrets governance.  
**Outcome**: consistent security, better traceability, improved compliance.

---

## ✅ Use case 3 — SaaS observability & reliability

**Problem**: limited visibility, hard‑to‑diagnose incidents, unstable performance.  
**Approach**: define KPIs, centralized logs, alerting and runbooks.  
**Outcome**: faster detection, fewer incidents, improved resilience.
